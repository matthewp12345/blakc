Rules

  1. Cards from 2-10 are worth their face value. Face cards are worth 10. Aces are worth 11 or 1
  2. If your hand is closer to 21 than the dealer's, you win an equal amount to your bet. If your hand is equal to 21, it is called a “blackjack” or a “natural” and you automatically win
  3. If the dealer's hand is closer to 21 than yours, you lose your entire bet. If your hand becomes over 21 after a hit, it is called a “bust” and you automatically lose your bet
  4. If your hand and the dealer's hand are equal, you do not win or lose anything.
  5. Soft 17 is another way of describing a blackjack hand worth 17 which includes an Ace, where the Ace counted either as 1 or 11
